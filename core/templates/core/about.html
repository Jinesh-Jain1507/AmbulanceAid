About InstaCare

How to Book ?

Booking an ambulance with us is simple. You can call our emergency hotline or use our user-friendly mobile app or website to request assistance. We also work closely with healthcare providers and insurance companies to streamline the process.

Just fill the details related to the patient and your location and the best available Ambulance will arrive at your door step at the earliest and take the patient to the most suitable hospital nearby.

Your safety and well-being are our top priorities, and we are committed to providing the highest level of care during transportation.

Don't hesitate to contact us in case of a medical emergency. Your life matters, and we are here to help you during critical times.
